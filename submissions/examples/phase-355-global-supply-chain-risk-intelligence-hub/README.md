# Phase 355: Global Supply Chain Risk Intelligence Hub

## Overview

The **Global Supply Chain Risk Intelligence Hub** is an enterprise supply chain resilience platform identifying disruptions, logistics risks, and operational bottlenecks. This platform provides end-to-end visibility across the entire supply chain network with predictive risk assessment.

## Features

### 1. Supplier Risk Index
Comprehensive supplier risk assessment with financial health monitoring, geopolitical risk analysis, and capacity scoring. Real-time alerts for supplier disruptions and alternative sourcing recommendations.

### 2. Logistics Monitoring
End-to-end shipment tracking with carrier performance metrics, route optimization, and delivery time predictions. Interactive visualizations of global logistics networks.

### 3. Inventory Intelligence
Real-time inventory visibility across all warehouses and distribution centers with demand forecasting, safety stock optimization, and replenishment recommendations.

### 4. Shipment Visibility
Complete shipment tracking from origin to destination with milestone tracking, exception alerts, and delivery performance analytics.

### 5. Trade Analytics
Comprehensive trade data analysis with tariff impact assessment, compliance monitoring, and cost optimization opportunities.

### 6. Executive Overview
C-suite dashboard with key supply chain metrics, risk exposure, and strategic recommendations for supply chain resilience.
