# Phase 367: Climate Intelligence Decision Platform

## Overview

The **Climate Intelligence Decision Platform** is a scientific climate intelligence platform tracking environmental indicators and predictive climate models. This platform provides researchers and policymakers with tools for understanding and responding to climate change.

## Features

### 1. Climate Simulations
Climate model visualization with scenario comparison, projection analysis, and uncertainty quantification.

### 2. Emission Trends
Greenhouse gas emissions tracking with source analysis, reduction tracking, and compliance monitoring.

### 3. Temperature Analytics
Global temperature monitoring with anomaly detection, trend analysis, and regional breakdowns.

### 4. Risk Assessment
Climate risk evaluation with vulnerability mapping, impact scoring, and adaptation planning.

### 5. Policy Insights
Policy analysis tools with mitigation strategies, cost-benefit analysis, and recommendation engine.

### 6. Global Monitoring
Worldwide climate monitoring with satellite data integration, ground station networks, and oceanic measurements.
