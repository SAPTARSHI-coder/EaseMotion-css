How it works

  • Cards have container-type: inline-size to establish a containment context.
  • @container (min-width: ...) queries adjust font-size, line-height, and padding.
  • The wider card (.cq-card--wide) spans 2 columns, triggering a different container size than the others.
  • The media-query section uses standard @media — all cards change uniformly.
  • Resize the browser to see the contrast: CQ cards adapt individually, MQ cards adapt together.
