min() and max() Examples

Individual min() and max() functions for one-sided clamping.

min(90%, 30rem)

This card caps its width at 30rem. On narrow screens it takes 90% of the parent; on wide screens it stays at 30rem. The min() function selects the smaller value, preventing over-expansion.

max(16rem, 50%)

This card ensures a minimum width of 16rem. On wide screens it takes 50% of the parent; on narrow screens it never shrinks below 16rem. The max() function selects the larger value, preventing over-contraction.
