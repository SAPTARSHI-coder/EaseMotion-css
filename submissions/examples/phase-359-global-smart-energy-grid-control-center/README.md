# Phase 359: Global Smart Energy Grid Control Center

## Overview

The **Global Smart Energy Grid Control Center** is an advanced grid operations platform monitoring electricity distribution, renewable integration, and resilience. This platform provides utilities with comprehensive tools for managing modern power grids with distributed energy resources.

## Features

### 1. Grid Topology
Interactive visualization of transmission and distribution networks with substation status, transformer loads, and line capacity monitoring.

### 2. Load Balancing
Real-time load balancing with demand response coordination, peak shaving optimization, and load forecasting.

### 3. Renewable Generation
Solar and wind generation monitoring with weather correlation, storage integration, and grid stability analysis.

### 4. Outage Detection
Automated outage detection with fault location identification, customer impact assessment, and restoration coordination.

### 5. Demand Forecasting
AI-powered demand prediction with weather integration, historical analysis, and scenario planning.

### 6. Energy Analytics
Comprehensive energy analytics with consumption patterns, cost analysis, and efficiency recommendations.
