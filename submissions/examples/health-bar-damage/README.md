# Health Bar Damage Animation

## What does this do?
An animated health bar that shows damage chunks with a flashing damage number.

## How is it used?
Add `.healthbar` container with `.hp`, `.damage`, and `.labels` elements.

## Why is it useful?
Health bar animations provide crucial game feedback, communicating damage impact clearly.
