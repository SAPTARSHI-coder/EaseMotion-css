# Phase 358: Global ESG Intelligence Observatory

## Overview

The **Global ESG Intelligence Observatory** is an executive sustainability intelligence platform measuring environmental, social, and governance performance. This platform provides comprehensive ESG metrics and analytics for organizations committed to sustainable business practices.

## Features

### 1. Carbon Metrics
Real-time carbon footprint tracking with emissions inventory, reduction targets, and progress visualization. Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions monitoring with industry benchmarks.

### 2. ESG Scorecards
Comprehensive ESG scoring with E, S, and G pillars broken down into sub-metrics. Historical trends, peer comparisons, and improvement recommendations.

### 3. Sustainability Roadmap
Strategic sustainability planning with milestone tracking, initiative management, and investment ROI analysis for ESG projects.

### 4. Compliance Monitoring
Regulatory compliance tracking for environmental regulations, reporting frameworks (GRI, SASB, TCFD), and certification maintenance.

### 5. Impact Analytics
Social and environmental impact measurement with data-driven storytelling, stakeholder reporting, and narrative development tools.

### 6. Executive Reports
Board-ready ESG reports with automated data aggregation, visualization templates, and regulatory filing support.

## Technical Implementation

- Entry Animations: em-fade-in, em-slide-up, em-grow
- Continuous Animations: em-pulse, em-leaf
- Interaction Animations: em-hover-lift, em-hover-scale

### Responsive Design
- Desktop (1200px+): Executive dashboard with detailed analytics
- Tablet (768px-1200px): Simplified executive view
- Mobile (<768px): KPI summary and alerts

### Dark Mode Support
Sustainable-focused dark theme with nature-inspired accents.

### Accessibility Compliance
WCAG 2.1 guidelines with clear data visualization.
