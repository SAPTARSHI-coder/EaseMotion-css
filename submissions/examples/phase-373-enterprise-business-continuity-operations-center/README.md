# Phase 373: Enterprise Business Continuity Operations Center

## Overview

The **Enterprise Business Continuity Operations Center** is a resilience platform ensuring business continuity through incident management and recovery coordination. This platform provides organizations with tools for maintaining operations during disruptions.

## Features

### 1. Incident Dashboard
Incident tracking with severity classification, status updates, and impact assessment.

### 2. Recovery Timeline
Recovery process visualization with milestone tracking, resource allocation, and completion forecasting.

### 3. Risk Register
Comprehensive risk management with likelihood assessment, impact analysis, and mitigation tracking.

### 4. Service Dependencies
Service dependency mapping with critical path analysis, single point of failure identification, and resilience scoring.

### 5. Business Impact
Impact analysis with financial loss estimation, customer affect assessment, and recovery priority determination.

### 6. Executive Resilience KPIs
Resilience metrics with recovery time objectives, recovery point objectives, and continuity readiness scores.
